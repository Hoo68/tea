About Tea Sage

Our Journey / 我们的旅程

Tea Sage started its journey in 2012 with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide premium, wellness-focused teas to customers seeking balance and healing in their busy lives. Over the years, we’ve faced numerous challenges, from sourcing the finest tea leaves to navigating the impact of the MCO in 2020, which significantly affected businesses worldwide. Yet, despite these obstacles, our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has never wavered. By 2024, we’ve proudly served over 200,000 customers, offering teas that support digestive health, reduce dampness, and promote overall wellness. Tea Sage isn’t just a brand; it’s a story of resilience, passion, and a deep connection to the tradition of tea culture. / 茶夫子自2012年起步，秉持着简单而强大的使命——为客户提供优质的养生茶，帮助他们在忙碌的生活中找到平衡与健康。在过去的几年里，我们面对了诸多挑战，从精心挑选最优质的茶叶到应对2020年MCO（行动管制令）对全球商业的影响。尽管如此，我们始终未曾动摇对品质和客户满意度的承诺。到2024年，我们已经为超过20万位顾客提供了帮助消化、减轻湿气、提升整体健康的茶叶。茶夫子不仅仅是一个品牌，它是一个关于坚韧、激情与对茶文化深厚情感的故事。

Our Mission / 我们的使命

At Tea Sage, we are committed to crafting wellness teas that support not only your physical health but also your mental wellbeing. Our mission is to provide a holistic approach to wellness by offering high-quality teas that nourish the body, mind, and soul. We believe that nature holds the key to health, and through our carefully selected blends, we aim to bring the ancient wisdom of tea to modern life. Whether you seek to strengthen your digestive system, boost your energy levels, or simply unwind after a long day, Tea Sage is here to support your journey to a healthier lifestyle. / 在茶夫子，我们致力于打造既有益于身体健康，也有助于心理健康的养生茶。我们的使命是通过提供高质量的茶叶，支持全面的健康，让身体、心灵与灵魂都得到滋养。我们相信，大自然蕴藏着健康的秘密，通过我们精心挑选的茶叶配方，我们将古老的茶道智慧带入现代生活。无论是增强消化系统功能、提升能量，还是在一天的劳累之后放松心情，茶夫子都将是您通往更健康生活方式的伙伴。

Why Choose Us? / 为什么选择我们?

Tea Sage stands out in the tea market for its unwavering commitment to quality. We handpick the finest ingredients from around the world and blend them with traditional techniques to create wellness teas that are not only effective but also delicious. Our products are made with convenience in mind, offering easy-to-use tea bags that are perfect for busy individuals. Whether you’re at home, at work, or on the go, you can enjoy a cup of Tea Sage’s premium wellness tea at any time. We understand that your health is important, and we want to make it easier for you to make the right choice for your wellbeing. / 茶夫子在茶叶市场中脱颖而出，因其对品质的坚持不懈。我们精心挑选全球最优质的茶叶原料，并结合传统工艺，调配出既有效又美味的养生茶。我们的产品注重便捷性，采用方便的茶包设计，适合忙碌的现代人群。无论您是在家、工作还是外出，都能随时享用茶夫子的优质养生茶。我们明白您的健康至关重要，我们希望能让您更轻松地为自己的健康做出正确的选择。
